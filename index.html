Why banana fiber?

Waste in. Heirlooms out.

After every banana harvest, the plant's trunk is cut down and discarded. We turn that fiber into some of the strongest natural weaves on earth.

  • Stronger than it looks

    Banana fiber is tougher than jute and naturally tear-resistant — baskets keep their shape for years of daily use.

  • Zero new crops, zero plastic

    Made entirely from harvest leftovers. No extra land, water, or pesticides — and fully biodegradable at end of life.

  • Fair pay for skilled hands

    Woven by artisan cooperatives in Uganda and Tanzania who earn fair, stable wages — most of them women-led.

  • Beautiful by nature

    No dyes needed. Every weave carries the warm, sun-cured tones of the fiber itself — no two pieces identical.
